

## SYNDICATE — AI Defence Drone Command Center
### Qatar Ministry of Defense — Tactical Prototype

**Theme & Branding:**
- Dark military aesthetic: deep black backgrounds (#0a1007, #15210f, #1f3217) transitioning through rich greens (#2a431f, #355427, #3f642f, #4a7537, #6aa84f)
- Accent colors: amber/gold (#D4A537) for alerts and highlights, red (#DC2626) for threats
- Typography: bold uppercase headers, monospace for telemetry data
- The "SYNDICATE" branding images will be embedded as logo assets on the login screen
- Military-grade UI: sharp corners on data panels, glowing green indicators, scan-line effects
